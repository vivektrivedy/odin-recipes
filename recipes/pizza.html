Pizza

Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A small pizza is sometimes called a pizzetta.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C)
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a lightly floured surface
  3. Transfer dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce
  6. Add your desired toppings
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly
  8. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving
